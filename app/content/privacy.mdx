# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** February 12, 2026

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect
it, and how we use it when you visit our website or book a session with The
Dice Bard.

## Information We Collect

- **Contact details** you provide (name, email, phone).
- **Booking details** (session type, date, time, and preferences).
- **Payment info** is handled by our payment processor. We do not store full
  credit card numbers.
- **Website usage data** (pages viewed, device/browser info, and approximate
  location) collected through cookies and analytics tools.

## How We Use Information

- To **schedule and deliver** sessions.
- To **respond** to questions and support requests.
- To **improve** our services and website.
- To **send updates** about bookings or policy changes.

## Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar tools to keep the site working and to understand how
visitors use it. You can block cookies in your browser, but some features may
not function properly.

## Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only when needed:

- **Service providers** (email, scheduling, payments, and analytics).
- **Legal requirements** (if required by law or to protect our rights).
